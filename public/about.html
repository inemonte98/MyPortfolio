About Me

Hello World!

I finished my degree in Hospitality Management in 2019. And I don't know if you heard about it or not, but a few months later the entire world shut down. Sinse I wanted to work in hotels in order to deliver the best exerience to my customer and I couldn't, I had to start looking for a different course on my professional life.

That is when I found Product Management, and it was almost love at first sight. I could still be close to customers and deliver something that would make their lifes better.

I started to learn how to code, because I wanted to understand how to better communicate with Developers on how to explain to them the vision of the product and how we could build it together. For me a team that is united and works well toghether will have a much better chance to deliver a product with quality and value.

I know that my University degree has nothing to do with what I do nowadays, however I belive that learning is something we should do until the end of our days. Whether is in our personal life and grow as a human being, or in our work and develop as a professional.
